Twelve Finger Salute
The entire party singles out a creature for ego death. All Stormbringers lift their hands up and extend the middle finger, displaying it to that creature and dealing 66d6 points of non-canon psychic damage.
Extension: Power Word Immolate - All Stormbringers hurl insult after insult while maintaining their gestures.
Step 1: Simply extend the middle finger.
Step 2: Give it some emotion!
Step 3: Now give it more 'zazz! Make it yours!
